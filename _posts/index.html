About

Hello World! My name is Victor Diaz a spaniard guy who was born in Granada, Spain and now living in Barcelona. I studied Telecomunication Engineering and recently I finished an Interdisciplinary Master of Cognitive Systems and Interactive Media aka CSIM in the University Pompeu Fabra in Barcelona. I am really interested in human emotions, in how the brain works, new ways of education, in data visualizations, new ways of interfacing with computers, photography, cinema, more humanized architecture and many more! And even better if everything is seasoned with open source and free culture!! Too many things isn't it? It could be but somehow all of them are interconnected :) Here you can find some of the projects I did. They are not too many and not something that can be called amazing but I enjoyed a lot doing them, specially when my friends got involved. My biggest passion is to travel, it always reminds me that my culture is not the only one in the world and there is many ways of thinking and seeing about the same thing. This is the beauty of this world, an heritage we should keep, and yes I'm writing this in english and not in my native language, spanish :) I love languages as well and although I do not speak any perfectly I try to do it in English, French, German and Japanese apart my mother tongue Spanish. Here some places where I have been (by visiting order)!! Spain, Portugal, France, England, Ireland, Italy, Japan, Switzerland, Germany, Austria, Czech Republic, The Netherlands, Belgium, Morocco, Hungary, Poland, Slovakia, Sweden. And places where I lived Granada, Jaen, Madrid, Barcelona (Spain), Montpellier and Puylaurens (France), Bern Switzerland) and Tokyo (Japan)
